About Me

My journey into tech started with a career in law specializing in intellectual property. As I explored the different facets of copyright, trademark and patents, I realized that my facisnation with technology was deep-rooted. I wanted to learn how to become one of the developers I admired so well. That curiosity then opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I've made a full switch to front-end development with my company SweetSpot.Tech, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.
